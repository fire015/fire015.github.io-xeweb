About

Hi there, I'm Jason. For the past 12 years I have worked in various software engineering leadership roles. I've been lucky enough to work for some fantastic companies, including Facebook and BP.

After a while, I started to feel like I wanted to do something more. I wanted to help startups navigate the technical challenges they face as they grow and scale. I realized that I had a lot of knowledge and experience to offer and I wanted to put it to use helping others.

As a technical advisor I work with startups of all sizes and stages, helping them develop their technical strategies and overcome challenges - from hiring great engineers to building an MVP.

I have a knack for explaining complex technical concepts in a way that is easy for non-technical stakeholders to understand.

I'd love to hear from you if this is something you're interested in.
